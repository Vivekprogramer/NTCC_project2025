Amity University Patna

Lost & Found Portal

This website is developed as part of the NTCC project to help students and staff at Amity University Patna report and recover lost or found belongings easily.

Purpose

The Lost & Found Portal provides a simple and secure way to report misplaced items and to notify the community when items are found. Our goal is to ensure that belongings are returned to their rightful owners quickly and efficiently.
